#Animation

Project Brief

Chinese Zodiacs is a senior project that requires me to complete a 3-minute animation within one semester. I choose the theme of the Chinese Zodiac to showcase traditional Chinese culture.

My Role

  • Planned the overall storyline and organized the project timeline
  • Created the animation storyboard
  • Created character designs for the 12 Zodiacs
  • Researched and designed the scenes for the short animation
  • Handled the post-production animation, film editing, and sound effect integration
