Emoogle

#Website

Project Overview

Emoogle is a keyword-based emoji input tool designed to help users quickly and accurately find the emojis they need. Since the product already has a clear positioning and functional logic, the focus of this design is on optimizing the "visual language + information structure" website layout.

Project Goal

Simplify the information hierarchy of the webpage layout to help users quickly understand and try the product.

My Role

  • Planning the overall page structure and information hierarchy
  • Designing the website and providing the necessary visual assets and components
  • Communicating with the development team to prioritize and address design details, ensuring accurate visual implementation

Insights

Understanding the Problem

Entry Point

Since the project already has a mature product form and functional logic, the design's main goal is to optimize it from the perspective of information priority and visual communication. The page structure primarily focuses on guiding users, emphasizing Emoogle's features and download entry, while using simple interactions to reduce the understanding threshold.

Insight #1: Emotion

Visual design is not just about typography and colors; it’s also about conveying the product's personality. Emoogle, being closely related to emojis, naturally carries an emotional tone. Therefore, I used bright colors, dynamic elements, and emoji icons to enhance the product’s impression of being "light, fun, and smart."

Insight #2: Rhythm

The rhythm of information presentation is crucial. By creating a clear page structure and browsing path, the goal was to help users grasp the key points in the shortest amount of time.

Design

Layout Design

①The banner area on the homepage uses large fonts and a concise slogan to convey the product's core selling points；②A product demo component with interactivity was designed to directly demonstrate the product's functionality；③By using high-contrast text and background images, a visual focus is created, allowing users to quickly identify and click the "Get App" button.

This section is the product details display area: ④An interactive product experience component where users can directly experience the Emoogle App’s features；⑤It displays the product’s key features；⑥It provides detailed usage instructions. This area is designed to help users understand the product and how to use it

⑦Dynamic GIFs were used to enhance the visual experience and also showcase Emoogle’s unique style；⑧Different pricing plans are shown so that users can choose based on their needs；⑨The FAQ section provides common answers to questions, helping users quickly find information and resolve any concerns.
