Project Overview

E3A Healthcare, a med-tech startup from NUS, focuses on non-invasive, smart maternal and infant care. As it expands globally, the new website helps share its vision and technology with users, investors, and partners worldwide.

Project Goal

Designed a clean, intuitive website that reflects E3A’s brand tone and clearly presents its product value. Beyond improving user experience, the site acts as a vital touchpoint for E3A’s global presence and outreach.

My Role

  • Planned the overall site structure and information hierarchy
  • Designed the interface and created all necessary visual assets and components
  • Built the site using the Squarespace platform, including HTML/CSS component development
  • Presented progress updates to the CEO and marketing lead at key stages, iterating based on their feedback

1.Discovery

Understanding the Needs, Shaping the Strategy

Initial Planning

At the start of the project, I was tasked with designing a website that reflected the brand, showcased products, and supported marketing goals. Beyond visuals, the focus was on building a clear structure and user journey—helping visitors like investors, partners, professionals, and job seekers quickly understand the company and easily get in touch.

In line with the company’s request, the site was built on Squarespace. Through discussions with the CEO and marketing team, we aligned on the key priorities: emphasizing brand professionalism and enhancing product visibility. I then created the sitemap to serve as a strategic guide.

Sitemap

The site was divided into five key sections: Home (brand and solutions), About Us (company background), Products (showcase), News (updates), and Contact Us (map and form). It includes a bilingual switch (English/Chinese) and a footer with quick links, ensuring a consistent and efficient user experience, laying the groundwork for the design stage.
