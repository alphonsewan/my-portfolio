About Me

Hi there! I'm a UI/UX designer who is always curious about new things and loves learning about interesting topics. I see design as a way of discovering and creating through observing and understanding the world around us. My goal is to help people better understand and use the design in the world to solve problems.

My Skills

UI/UX Design

Specialize in UI/UX design for websites, web applications, and mobile apps. My process includes early-stage customization of project themes and styles, establishing design systems when necessary, and creating UI design components along with MVP design. In later stages, I focus on optimizing designs based on testing feedback and developing low to high-fidelity models, including annotating UI design drafts.

Motion Design

Specialize in creating dynamic animations, including animated graphic elements and logo animations, suitable for UI projects or standalone applications. I also produce less complex motion graphics (MG) animations, primarily using Adobe After Effects.

Graphic Design

Familiar with graphic design and typographic design, with a focus on creating and designing vector elements. Capable of handling logo design, slide decks design, posters, and media post layouts. Additionally, there is a personal interest in learning 3D techniques.

Other
