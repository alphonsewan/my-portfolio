OpenResume

An open-source online ATS-friendly resume generator WebApp

#WebApp

Project Brief

OpenResume is an open-source online resume generator WebApp designed for ease of use. With minimal learning required, users can quickly create a professional, ATS-compliant resume.

The Problem

For users who urgently need a resume but lack familiarity with formatting or haven't updated it in a while, creating an ATS-compliant resume quickly can be challenging, leading to missed opportunities.

Goal

  • Simple and easy to understand: Ensure users can easily comprehend and use each feature
  • Quick: Ensure key processes are completed in just a few simple steps, minimizing unnecessary actions
  • Design style: Ensure the style conveys professionalism while visually guiding users through the process

My Role

  • Work with developers to define overall design requirements and project timeline, and quickly create an MVP design in the early stages
  • Use Figma to create wireframes and prototypes to ensure design accuracy and visual effectiveness
  • Present prototypes to developers and a selected group of users to gather feedback and insights, and begin initial implementation
  • Iterate and improve the prototype based on feedback to ensure the final design meets user needs and expectations

Step I

Understand the goal of the project

Here are some directions derived from market research and discussions with the founder regarding the OpenResume project:

Registration required

Some similar web applications focus more on personalized services and commercial aspects, requiring users to register an account or go through a series of steps before they can achieve their goal of generating a resume

Lack of live preview and fixed templates

Some excellent resume editors require account registration or multiple steps before users can start editing, while user-friendly ones lack real-time preview and have fixed template styles

Insight #1: Convenience

The core users of OpenResume are those who want to quickly obtain a standard resume, so we minimize the number of steps to reduce user operation and cognitive burden.

Insight #2: Interaction & Visibility

Traditional resume generators require multiple steps to preview the generated resume, while OpenResume provides real-time rendering, allowing users to see immediate feedback on their actions, improving the editing experience.

Insight #3: Flexibility

The OpenResume editor provides customizable layouts, fonts, and colors to cater to different visual style needs. Since this project is an open-source application, we can implement personalized UI components.

Step II

Wireframe

The homepage design consists of five areas. Upon entering the website, a brief introduction and an entry point are displayed, with a demo on the right side. Then, users will come to a suggested tutorial section, which not only shows users how to use the tool but also demonstrates the convenience of the application.

The remaining three areas focus on highlighting the advantages of OpenResume, showcasing real user experiences, and providing a clear comparison chart of similar applications, allowing users to transparently and objectively understand relevant information.

As for another important area—the resume editor section—it adopts a left-right layout. On the left side, there is a card-based design where users can add corresponding cards (personal information, work experience, skills, etc.) and input data. The real-time preview on the right side will instantly display the information within the resume template.
